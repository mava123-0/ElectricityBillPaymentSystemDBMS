Solar Power Purchase Agreement

The customer should have Solar Power Panels. On joining the SPPA, the customer get subsidies based on the number of units provided the customer. For every unit of electricity supplied by the customer, amount subsidized based on the current electricity rate from the monthly bill of the customer.
